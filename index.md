# Aquaculture Lab

The Kwasek Aquaculture Lab at the {% link="https://colsa.unh.edu/biological-sciences", text="University of New Hampshire"%} focuses on supporting the development of more sustainable intensive aquaculture practices. Over 90% of our research has direct application to the aquaculture industry, both fish farmers as well as feed manufacturers. We recognize the following main areas in our work: 1) modification of fish, 2) feeds, and 3) feeding methods that help fish thrive in more sustainable environments. All these areas stand on four research pillars that our lab represents: nutritional and environmental programming, gut healing, and larval nutrition.
